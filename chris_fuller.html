Please Welcome Chris Fuller to the Kings III Marketing Team

We’re excited to welcome Chris Fuller to our Marketing Team and the greater Kings III Team. Chris Fuller is joining the Southeast team as the new BDM of the Jacksonville market. Chris is a graduate of North Carolina A&T State University, where he earned a B.S. in Business Administration with a concentration in Marketing—a foundation that has supported a successful career in banking and B2B sales.

Committed to service, Chris has volunteered as a financial literacy instructor, helping young people build confidence with money and plan for brighter futures. He is also a proud member of Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity, Inc., reflecting his dedication to leadership, service, and community.

Outside of work, Chris enjoys spending time with his family, especially his two sons and three grandchildren. You’ll often find him fishing or cruising the Caribbean.

Chris is excited to be part of the Kings III family, and we’re thrilled to have him on board. Please join me in giving him a warm welcome!
